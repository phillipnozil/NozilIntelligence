About us & Future Goals

2023

founded

Nozil Intelligence Technologies was officially founded after years of providing freelance web development, AVL (audio, visual, and lighting), and live-stream production services. Our founder decided to formalize the business to better serve churches, nonprofits, small businesses, and event organizers with custom solutions.

2024

Building Strong Relationships

We quickly gained traction, offering tailored solutions for a variety of clients. With a focus on user-friendly web design, seamless AV integration, and high-quality live-stream experiences, we established ourselves as a trusted partner in the tech space.

2025

Goals: Expanding Capabilities

Nozil Intelligence Technologies plans to launch a suite of AI-driven tools tailored for churches and small businesses. This year will focus on enhancing customer outreach and solidifying partnerships in the tech and nonprofit sectors.

2026

Goals: Community-Centered Growth

Expanding into international markets will take center stage, with dedicated efforts to localize services for diverse communities. Additionally, we aim to develop a mentorship program for budding entrepreneurs in technology.

2027

Goals: Advancing Innovation

Innovation will drive our focus as we integrate sustainable practices into all aspects of our operations. By this year, we will unveil new products designed to empower underserved communities through affordable tech solutions.

2028

Goals: Going Global

Our goal is to establish research and development hubs to foster groundbreaking advancements in software and AV technology. This year will also see the launch of an annual tech summit to connect leaders across industries.

2029

Goals: Driving Sustainability

Scaling up operations globally, we aim to introduce remote support services that ensure accessibility for all clients. Concurrently, we will collaborate with educational institutions to promote STEM education in marginalized areas.

2030

Goals: Transforming the Industry

By this year, Nozil Intelligence Technologies envisions becoming a global leader, driving innovation and positive change in the industries we serve. We will measure our success by the tangible impact made on our clients and communities worldwide.
